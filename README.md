## 1. Project Title / Headline

**📊 HR Analytics: Workforce & Employee Performance Dashboard**  
A dynamic, interactive data visualization tool built to explore organizational workforce data—focusing on employee attrition drivers, performance ratings, satisfaction metrics, and departmental trends.

## 2. Short Description / Purpose

The HR Analytics Dashboard is a visually engaging and analytical Power BI report designed to help HR teams and management explore and compare employee metrics across different departments and job roles. The dashboard focuses on highlighting major workforce factors like attrition rates, average performance ratings, job satisfaction, salary hikes, and work-life balance. This tool is intended for use by HR analysts, business leaders, and data-driven strategists who seek to understand workforce health, improve retention, and optimize employee performance.

## 3. Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
* **📊 Power BI Desktop** – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
* **📂 Power Query** – Data transformation and cleaning layer used for reshaping, cleaning, and preparing the raw HR data.
* **🧠 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used for calculated measures (e.g., Attrition Rate, Avg Performance, High Performer %), dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.
* **📝 Data Modeling** – Relationships established to enable seamless cross-filtering and metric aggregation across the dashboard pages.
* **📁 File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` / `.pdf` for dashboard previews.

## 4. Data Source

**Source:** AI-Generated Synthetic HR Dataset.  
A custom, AI-generated dataset created to simulate a realistic corporate environment. It contains data on nearly 1,500 mock employees, including comprehensive details on their demographics (Age, Gender), roles (Department, Job Role), compensation (Monthly Income, Percent Salary Hike), performance (Performance Rating, Training Times), and engagement (Job Satisfaction, Environment Satisfaction, Work-Life Balance). 

## 5. Features / Highlights

* **Business Problem**  
High employee turnover and inconsistent performance tracking cost companies significant time and money. HR teams and leadership often lack an intuitive way to identify the root causes of attrition or understand the complex relationship between compensation, job satisfaction, and employee performance using raw spreadsheet data. Key questions such as: *Which departments have the highest flight risks? Are our top performers compensated fairly? Does work-life balance impact performance?* … are difficult to answer quickly without visual analytics.

* **Goal of the Dashboard**  
To deliver an interactive visual tool that:
  * Enables users to monitor workforce health and explore employee data globally across the company.
  * Supports strategic decisions regarding retention programs, compensation reviews, and training investments.
  * Uncovers trends in attrition, performance, and employee satisfaction by department and role.

* **Walkthrough of Key Visuals**  
  * **Key KPIs (Top Row):** Total Employees, Attrition Count, Attrition Rate, Avg Age, Avg Salary, and Avg Performance Rating.
  * **Left Filter Panel:** An interactive slicer pane lets users filter all visuals by Department, Job Role, Gender, Age Group, and Performance Rating.
  * **Attrition by Department & Demographics (Donut/Bar Charts):** Displays the distribution of employee turnover to identify high-risk areas.
  * **Average Performance by Department (Bar Chart):** Compares performance scores across Research & Development, Sales, and Human Resources.
  * **Income vs. Performance (Scatter Plot):** Plots Monthly Income against Performance Rating to visually explore if higher pay correlates with higher performance.
  * **Satisfaction by Rating (Grouped Bar Chart):** Compares Job Satisfaction and Environment Satisfaction across different performance tiers.
  * **Training & Experience Impact (Column Chart):** Tracks how variables like Total Working Years and Training Times Last Year affect employee outcomes.

* **Business Impact & Insights**  
  * **Retention Strategies:** HR teams can pinpoint departments or age groups with low satisfaction and high attrition to implement targeted retention and wellness programs.
  * **Compensation Review:** Management can analyze the Income vs. Performance visuals to ensure high performers are being compensated fairly compared to their peers.
  * **Performance Management:** Identify if specific roles require more training hours or better work-life balance interventions to boost overall performance ratings.
  * **Workforce Planning:** Business leaders can understand demographic distributions and experience levels to plan for future hiring, promotions, and succession.
